About

Find Out More About Us

Sadhana Shibirs (Spiritual Retreats) are specially packaged programs conducted by Swami Mukundananda. They are held in various pilgrimage places or at his main ashram in Banara, Odisha, India. These shibirs provide an opportunity to learn and practice the spiritual knowledge under Swamiji’s guidance.

n today’s fast-paced life, each day there are deadlines to meet, goals to achieve, society to maintain, and commitments to fulfill. From school fees to electricity bills, from mundane household chores to emergency social-financial crisis, there is no end to the worldly woes in a common man’s life. In such a situation, it becomes very difficult to take out time for devotional practice and meditation.

    Swamiji’s Sadhana Shibirs give you a perfect opportunity to get away from the world for a few days and, not only absorb your mind in devotion, but also to recharge yourself for upcoming challenges. The shibirs helps to understand and strengthen your relationship with God.
